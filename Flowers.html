THE FLOWER OF THE DAY

Is a Crossvine

This flower grows in vines that go soooo high up! The leaves are purplish green and the flower has such an interesting tubular shape. It reminds me of jelly fish or a sting ray, floating in the sea. Swimming graciously. The shape of this flower is as if it is in constant movement.

Makes me think of a long silky dress drapping down your body. I wish i could fit you inside this flower and put you in my pocket to take you wherever I go. I could take you to a park and present you to the other flowers.

I like how different this flower is!



Know the feeling you get when you look at her and think "Woooow that´s so unique"? Such a special flower! That is exactly how I felt when I realized you were special, and only a fraction of what I felt when I realized I loved you and only a fraction of what I feel whenever I talk to you, Honey.

You´re the flower from which the concept of a flower came from. Without you there are no gardens, there are no colors or perfumes!

You can tune in tomorrow for yet another flower and yet another love confession. For I will never run out of either of those. As long as i have you.
