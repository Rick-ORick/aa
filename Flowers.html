THE FLOWER OF THE DAY

Is a Busy Lizzie

It is a perennial flower with all of the delicate imponence of you. Looking at this flower reminds me of your hair. You know how graciosly your hair looks as it waves back and forth? As you move? As you put it in the shape of a cannon like last night? Haha! That same grace can be seen in this flower.

It is a flower that makes me think: stop everything. Just breathe and look at this garden. Because it is always in a garden - never alone. It is born in shrubs, meaning it´s acompanied by multiple others, clustered together. Creating some sort of tapestry you can´t help but admire.



So it´s more than a fitting flower! It is allegory of who you are. One of them is your hair, the other your eyes, the other your laughter, another your sense of humor. They´re all clustered together, forming the shape of your body, your soul and you. Let´s be like those flowers and stay clustered together, shall we? I give you my world, as a man, that you´ll always be in a garden - never alone.

You can tune in tomorrow for yet another flower and yet another love confession. For I will never run out of either of those. As long as i have you.
